What We Offer

Marketing and Advertising

Property marketing of your property is crucial to the success of your investment. With our experienced team, they have the ability to attract high quality tenants.

Tenant Screening and Placement

Effective tenant screening processes are crucial for the success of your investment. Our tenant selection policy ensures only qualified applicants will see your property.

Rent Collection

Get your rent on time every month. Our rent collection process makes it easier for the tenants as we would want the process to be smooth and efficient.

Inspections and Maintenance

Our scheduled property inspections and maintenance keep your investment property in good health. We will help you protect your investment, minimize costs and maximize income.

Evictions are unpleasant and at times may get messy. We will resort to this method if all protocols have been exhausted in dealing with a difficult tenant.
